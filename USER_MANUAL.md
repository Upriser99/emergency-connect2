## 🚀 Getting Started

### First Time Setup

1. **Open the App**
   - Visit: `https://emergency-connect.vercel.app`
   - Or open `index.html` in your browser

2. **Allow Location Access**
   - Click "Allow" when prompted for location
   - This helps find nearby emergency services

3. **Accept Disclaimer**
   - Read and accept the disclaimer
   - This appears only once

4. **Choose Language**
   - Click 🌐 icon in header
   - Toggle between English (EN) and Hindi (HI)

## 🚨 Emergency Tools

### Quick Action Bar

Located below category grid, 6 quick action buttons:

#### 1. 🚨 Siren
- **Purpose**: Alert people nearby
- **How to use**:
  1. Click Siren button
  2. Emergency sound plays
  3. Click "Stop" to turn off
- **Note**: Works offline, no internet needed

#### 2. 🆘 SOS
- **Purpose**: Emergency call to 112
- **How to use**: Click SOS button
- **Result**: Immediately calls national emergency number

#### 3. 📞 Numbers
- **Purpose**: Quick access to all emergency numbers
- **How to use**:
  1. Click Numbers button
  2. See all 7 emergency numbers
  3. Tap any number to call

**Emergency Numbers**:
- **112** - National Emergency (All services)
- **100** - Police
- **101** - Fire Brigade
- **102** - Ambulance
- **108** - Ambulance (Alternate)
- **1091** - Women's Helpline
- **1098** - Child Helpline

#### 4. 📍 Share Location
- **Purpose**: Share your location in emergency
- **How to use**:
  1. Click Share button
  2. Choose sharing method:
     - WhatsApp
     - SMS
     - Copy link
  3. Send to emergency contact

#### 5. 🔦 Flashlight (Coming Soon)
- Future feature for phone flashlight control

#### 6. 📱 SMS (Coming Soon)

## 🔧 Troubleshooting

### Location Not Detected

**Problem**: "Location unavailable" message

**Solutions**:
1. **Enable Location Services**:
   - Settings → Privacy → Location Services → ON
   - Allow browser location access

2. **Check Browser Permissions**:
   - Click 🔒 icon in address bar
   - Set Location to "Allow"

3. **Use WiFi/Mobile Data**:
   - Location works better with internet

### Map Not Loading

**Problem**: Map view shows blank

**Solutions**:
1. **Check Internet Connection**:
   - Map tiles need internet
   - Switch to List view if offline

2. **Refresh Page**:
   - Press Ctrl + Shift + R (hard refresh)

3. **Clear Browser Cache**:
   - Settings → Privacy → Clear browsing data

### Services Not Showing

**Problem**: "No services found nearby"

**Solutions**:
1. **Expand Search Radius**:
   - App searches within 10km
   - Try different category

2. **Check Location**:
   - Ensure you're in Indore
   - App is designed for Indore city

3. **Use Static Data**:
   - App has fallback data
   - Works even if API fails

### Siren Not Playing

**Problem**: No sound when clicking Siren

**Solutions**:
1. **Check Volume**:
   - Increase device volume
   - Unmute if muted

2. **Browser Permissions**:
   - Some browsers block auto-play
   - Click button again

3. **Try Different Browser**:
   - Chrome/Firefox recommended
